## Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Primary Blue: `#2196F3` (Material Blue)
- Primary Light: `#64B5F6`
- Primary Dark: `#1976D2`

**Text Colors:**
- Primary Text: `#212121` (Dark Grey)
- Secondary Text: `#757575` (Grey)
- Hint Text: `#BDBDBD` (Light Grey)

**Background:**
- Default Background: `#FFFFFF` (White)
- Card Background: `#FAFAFA` (Off-white)

## Typography

**Headers:**
- App Title: Roboto, 24pt, Bold
- Section Headers: Roboto, 20pt, Medium

**Body:**
- Body Text: Roboto, 16pt, Regular
- Caption: Roboto, 14pt, Regular

## Interactive Elements

**Buttons:**
- Elevated Button: Blue background, white text
- Text Button: Blue text, transparent background
- Ripple Effect: Material ripple on tap

**App Bar:**
- Height: 56dp
- Background: Inverse Primary Color (Light Blue)
- Text: Dark text on light background

- Default Padding: 16dp
